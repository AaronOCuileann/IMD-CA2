Work Experience


During my work experience, I spent my six months working and learning with Fathom. Fathom creates innovative software solutions, using leading edge technologies, deployed in the Cloud. We work with technologies that help drive business value. We are full stack developers and normally use NodeJS to deliver APIs and ReactJS to deliver Progressive Web Applications. We have a deep understanding of Devops and practice CI/CD and engineer security in all our deliveries.

Over the period of my work experience I had learnt a lot from the other developers in the company and how a company goes through the process of development thoughtout a week. At the beginning of each week we have a metting to discuss the tasks that will be accomplished within the week, as they use an agile methodology in Fathom. They use task boards to discuss tasks and after this they decide on the tasks to be pulled across to be done. Every morning we also have a ten minute meeting to discuss what had been done the day before and what will be done during that day as well.

During my work experience with Fathom, I got the oppurtunity to use NodeJS and ReactJS to develop projects. One of the projects I was working on was Nualang, a website that allows students and teachers to create courses and bots for learning, as well as developing a generic admin app for future projects. Currently I am still working with Fathom part time while continuing on in my fourth year of college.

Projects


2nd Year Project - Loop

For my second year project, myself and two other students created a social media website, which also included an e-commerce section within the website, called Loop. We decided on this project as it would add a challenge to the project as we had never created a social media website before this. For this project we had used Django as our web framework, and then used Python as the programming language, while using css and bootstrap for the styling of the website. Our webite consisted of a login and logout, adding other members on the website, groupchats, customizing your profile, posting public and private posts, comments and likes on posts, and a section where you could purchase merchandise.

Software Development Project - Fresh Shop

FreshShop was a software development project we completed as a group during our third semester. We were learning how to use django as a web framework and used Python as the programming language for our project. FreshShop was an e-commerce website that would allow a user to login/logout of their account, choose products and purchase those products.
